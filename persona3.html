Margarette Jones

Age:

62

Occupation:

Retired, Head of Homeowner's Association, Volunteer Coordinator

About Margarette:

Margarette is an outspoken community advocate who loves baking and organizing functions and parties for her volunteering organization. In her down time, she enjoys knitting and being with her grandchildren. She suffers from arthritis, but she doesn't let that stop her from living her best life.


Goals:

  • To get volunteers for her organization.
  • To find like minded individuals to be around.
  • To live healthier and prolong life to watch grandkids grow up.
  • To help better the community.

Needs:

  • To feel a sense of accomplishment whenver her organization gives back to the community.
  • To find more volunteers for events that her organization holds.
  • To find companionship.

Pain Points:

  • It is hard to find volunteers willing to help.
  • She requires local volunteers as she doesn't want to go far away.
  • Low income, depends on pension.
