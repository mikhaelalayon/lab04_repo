Homepage

Problems

There are many volunteer opportunities, but the process to finding them is overwhelming due to the abundance of resources available online.


Problem Domain

  1. Hard to find volunteering opportunities that align with your interests and fit into your schedule
  2. No way to determine the legitimacy of volunteer opportunities
  3. Hard to keep track of volunteer hours and schedule
  4. Too many isolated volunteer resources online, but few consolidate every local volunteer resource into one site
  5. Current volunteer sites are difficult to navigate
